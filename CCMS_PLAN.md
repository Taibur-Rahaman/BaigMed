# 📋 Cursor Clinic Management System (CCMS) — Plan & Features

This plan selects the strongest capabilities from leading tools (Dentrix, Open Dental, Dentrix Ascend, Curve Dental, Eaglesoft, Dental Suite and local systems) into one clear plan for **Cursor Clinic Management System (CCMS)**.

**Implementation:** BaigDentPro is the codebase implementing CCMS.

---

## ✅ 1. Core System Goals

### a. Simple digital clinic record system

- Clear patient charts with history, treatment plans, imaging preview, and procedure codes.
- Quick click scheduling with timeline view and booking windows.
- Standard dental charting with tooth numbering.

### b. Billing, payments and inventory

- Billing templates for common dental procedures.
- Stock tracking for dental materials.
- Invoicing with receipt print and export.

### c. Reminders and communication

- SMS and WhatsApp appointment reminders automatically.
- Email summaries for patient visits.

### d. Cloud & offline support

- Sync records to cloud so multiple devices can share.
- Local offline mode so clinic still works if internet is disrupted.

---

## 🧠 2. Best Features Borrowed from Top Systems

### 🛠️ Administrative & Scheduling Tools

**From Dentrix & Curve Dental:**

- Drag-and-drop appointment calendar with color codes.
- Online booking for patients via simple link.

**From Open Dental:**

- Custom fields for unique clinic workflows or local codes.
- Flexible setup for appointment durations and resources (rooms, techs).

---

### 💳 Billing & Payments

**From Dentrix Ascend:**

- Automatic calculation of fees and recommended charges.
- Support for multiple payment types (cash, digital wallet, card).
- End-of-day totals and sales sheet.

**From local tools (e.g. ZilSoft Dentist PRO):**

- Prescription letters in local standard format.
- Local tax calculation and compliance.

---

### 📟 Clinical Charting & Imaging

**From Eaglesoft:**

- Detailed dental charting interface.
- Support for X-ray image integration.

**From Cloudify / Dental Suite:**

- Quick view images attached to patient record.
- Smooth user interface for adding notes.

---

### 📊 Reporting & Growth Tools

- Daily, weekly and monthly revenue reports.
- Patient retention statistics.
- Treatment plan success tracking.

---

## 📅 3. Suggested Rollout Plan for a Clinic

| Phase       | What To Do                                        | Timeline   |
| ----------- | ------------------------------------------------- | ---------- |
| **Phase 1** | Install system, enter basic info, set up users    | 1 week     |
| **Phase 2** | Enter patient database, configure services & fees | 1 week     |
| **Phase 3** | Train staff on schedules and billing              | 2–3 days   |
| **Phase 4** | Go live with reminders and online booking        | 1 day      |
| **Phase 5** | Review reports weekly and adjust workflows       | Ongoing    |

---

## 💰 4. Pricing Strategy

Three plans so clinics of different sizes can adopt easily:

### 🟦 Plan A – Starter

- Up to 3 users
- Scheduling, basic charting, SMS reminders
- Inventory tracking
- Cost: low monthly fee

### 🟩 Plan B – Standard

- All Starter features
- Cloud sync
- Billing and invoicing
- Online patient booking
- Cost: moderate monthly fee

### 🟥 Plan C – Premium

- All Standard features
- Advanced reports
- Imaging support
- Unlimited users
- Priority support
- Cost: higher monthly fee
